IOT Day 2: Setup is done

A journey of thousand miles starts with a single step. It looks like the setup phase is completed. Let's try the first program.
It finally appeared that the USB cable was responsible of my troubles. I learned that they are two kind of cables: * Those that only provide power supply * Those that provide the full wiring, allowing serial bus communication Guess what... So, after getting the proper cable, I finally heard the magic new USB device sound in Windows when connecting the Wemos. Excited, I opened Arduino and checked if the port was available... and yes it is ! ![Arduino port](https://ArnaudBuchholz.github.io/blog/post/iot-2/Arduino port.png) To validate the whole setup, I got a sample program from this [page](http://www.cnx-software.com/2016/03/22/getting-started-with-wemos-d1-mini-esp8266-board-dht-relay-shields/) and compiled it. ![Compiling...](https://ArnaudBuchholz.github.io/blog/post/iot-2/Compile.png) Then, I clicked the upload program, unplugged the device from the workstation and plugged it on the battery. Et voila !
