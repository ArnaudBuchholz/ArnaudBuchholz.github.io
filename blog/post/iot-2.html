IOT Day 2: Setup is done

A journey of thousand miles starts with a single step. It looks like the setup phase is completed.
It finally appeared that the USB cable was responsible of my troubles. I learned that they are two kind of cables: * Those that only provide power supply * Those that provide the full wiring, allowing serial bus communication Guess what... So, after getting the proper cable, I finally heard the magic new USB device sound in Windows when connecting the Wemos. Excited, I opened Arduino and checked if the port was available... and yes it is ! ![Arduino port](https://ArnaudBuchholz.github.io/blog/post/iot-2/Arduino port.png) BLINK EXAMPLE
