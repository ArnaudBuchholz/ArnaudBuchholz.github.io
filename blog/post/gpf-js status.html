Almost 3 years after presenting GPF-JS library, where is it now?
Almost 3 years ago, I presented the library by publishing the article [GPF-JS Presentation](http://gpf-js.blogspot.com/2016/01/gpf-js-presentation.html) and exposing the main ideas I wanted to develop. ## Version Since the presentation, [12 versions](https://github.com/ArnaudBuchholz/gpf-js#versions) were released. The most important milestones were: [0.1.5 The new core](http://gpf-js.blogspot.com/2016/12/release-015.html) fundation of the library [0.1.8 Interfaces](http://gpf-js.blogspot.com/2017/03/release-018.html) gpf.define with interfaces release optimization [0.2.2 gpf.require](https://gpf-js.blogspot.com/2017/11/release-022-gpfrequire.html) [0.2.3 Streams and Pipes](https://gpf-js.blogspot.com/2017/12/release-023-streams-and-pipes.html) [0.2.6 gpf.require.js](https://gpf-js.blogspot.com/2018/05/release-026-gpfrequirejs.html) ## Achievements https://gpf-js.blogspot.com/2018/01/my-own-require-implementation.html https://gpf-js.blogspot.com/2017/02/my-own-super-implementation.html https://gpf-js.blogspot.com/2016/12/my-own-jsdoc-plugin.html https://gpf-js.blogspot.com/2016/08/my-own-templates-implementation.html https://gpf-js.blogspot.com/2015/10/my-own-bdd-implementation.html Multi platform Development environment
